ABOUT US

Fly body is far from your ordinary gym that was founded in 2020 by two sisters Ola tunde & Ola itan . Their mission was to provide people with affordable access to quality exercise faciliities, build a community of gym enthusiast and provide a platform for like minded people to hold themselves accountable and build relationships.

FlyBody was created to help busy individuals who want to live a healthy lifestyle but due to certain life circumstances they are far from achieving this goal. We have all beeen there that is why we pride ourselves as not your ordinary gym rather we are a community established to assist you achieve those goals both virtually and in our live classes.
